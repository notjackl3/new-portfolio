May, 2020 - Content Creation & Video Editing

I started with editing Minecraft videos on Filmora. In 2021, I took video editing more seriously and started learning Adobe Premiere and After Effects. Finally, it paid off in summer of 2022 where one video blew up, reaching 10,000 views in a few days. Then I had videos with 100,000 views, finally 500,000 views, then the video that I spent 30 hours editing, peaked at 600,000 views.


September, 2022 - Vice-President of Create and Care

I joined the club as a member, but after a few days, the President promoted me because of my positive energy. All students really enjoyed the club, some of them even asked me to add friend them on Facebook! Following that, we took the hand-made products to sell them at seasonal events and generated $1,200+ in revenue.



October, 2022 - BISHCMC Wellbeing Committee Associate

Between Academic, Event, and Wellbeing, I applied to be a Wellbeing department!



November, 2022 - Design Technology Ambassador

I became the Design Technology Ambassador and hosted 3 welcoming events for students and parents. I introduced 150+ guests to the subject through live-actions, presentations, and interactive 3D softwares. We used Fusion360, Adobe Illustrator, and techniques such as aluminium die-casting, acrylic printing, wood-working, etc. After each event, the head techer always gave me a bar of chocolate to say thank you.



November, 2022 - Academic Mentoring

I was selected to be an academic mentor, where I would help students with their weekly homework. These sessions were hosted by the school and it can reach up to 30+ students. At the end of the year, I remembered being the only student who received gifts from students. I was given chocolate bars, a hand-written note, and coffee.



December, 2022 - BISHCMC Prefect Student

Out of 110 students, 30 was chosen as Prefect Students to be a role model for students in younger years.


September, 2023 - President of Design Now

I started my own club with 2 friends. We instructed 20+ students on how to use CAD software such as Fusion360, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, etc. Students asked for our help to produce products made of 3D printed acrylic and resin. Some others wanted to design with wood (one student chose to make a real-size knife!)
